Who We Are

About Us

Light Magicians from Eclectic Fields

VISUALANTIES is a collective of light magicians from eclectic fields of Art, Science, and Technology who band together to deliver quintessential experiences. Based in Vorarlberg, Austria, we combine cutting-edge technology with artistic creativity to create captivating visual experiences.

Our mission is to transform spaces and events through innovative LED systems that evoke powerful emotions, inspiring awe, joy, and wonder in everyone who experiences them. We believe in the transformative power of beauty and emotion, and we're committed to using our creativity to make a positive impact.

Our Services

LED Stage Design

Custom LED stage designs that transform performances and events with stunning visual effects. We provide turn-key experience design support from conception and brand intention to stage design and performance teams.

Learn More

Audio Reactive Installations

Real-time audio reactive installations that create moments of synesthesia, with LEDs pulsing to the beat and synchronized sensory experiences that engage multiple senses simultaneously.

Learn More

Interactive Installations

MQTT IOT distributed sensors and actuators that drive events, allowing moments of play for adults and children alike. Buttons, sliders, and touch screens can affect lights and projection mapping effects.
