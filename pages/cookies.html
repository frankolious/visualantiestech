2. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (such as your computer or mobile device) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to the owners of the site.

3. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: These cookies are necessary for the operation of the Website and enable basic functions such as page navigation and access to secure areas of the site. You cannot opt-out of these cookies.
  • Analytical/Performance Cookies: These cookies allow us to analyze how visitors use the Website and monitor its performance. This helps us improve the user experience and optimize our website for better performance.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies enable the Website to remember choices you make (such as your language preferences) and provide enhanced features and personalized content.
  • Targeting/Advertising Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver advertisements relevant to your interests. They may also be used to limit the number of times you see an advertisement and measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns.

4. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the functionality of the Website.
  • To analyze and measure the performance of the Website.
  • To personalize your experience and provide tailored content.
  • To serve targeted advertisements based on your browsing history and interests.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies used on the Website may be placed by third-party service providers, such as analytics providers or advertisers. These third parties may use cookies to collect information about your online activities across different websites and services.

6. Cookie Consent

By using the Website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in this policy. You can manage your cookie preferences and opt-out of certain cookies by adjusting your browser settings or by using the cookie consent banner provided on the Website.
